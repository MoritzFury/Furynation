About me

My name is Moritz also known as Fury. Born 1996 in Germany. I am a hobby musician, surveyor and streamer. On this site you will find my links to my social media and streaming platforms, collected works of interest in surveying and collected proof about a topic close to my heart which is possible lost civilizations during the ice age.

Why Ice Age civilizations

The reason why I chose that topic is that archeologists believe that they mostly have the history of humanity and civilizations figured out. That believe can create a dogma and a paradigm around our history. But to my opinion there is an overwhelming amount of clues and sites that made me doubt it. Under the thread Proof of civilizations you will find multiple sources and articles about that topic, archeological sites and what convinced me that there is more to our history than we currently believe.

Surveying

Surveying has been a fascination of mine for a long time. I have always been amazed by ancient maps and the art that went into surveying the areas and creates their respective maps. And since one of my hobbies also includes medieval fairs specifically represenation of the roman 1st century AD and 13th-15th century knights. For that I started researching the surveying methods throughout time and started collecting information about those methods. Also cartography and surveying has held an important part in some times throughout history. Under the thread of Surveying throughout time you will find the used methods and examples of surveying and cartography.
